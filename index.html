WORLD PHYGITAL COMMUNITY

Non-profit and non-commercial organization established in 2023 and headquartered in Hongkong unites pioneers of global phygital sports

OUR VISION

The PERFECT MATCH of physical and digital games and sport

OUR MISSION

We are MATCH-MAKERS. Our mission is to unite the physical and digital communities to create the PERFECT MATCH

OUR PURPOSE

To promote phygital sport and disciplines worldwide. To create and guard the rules and regulations of phygital disciplines. To build the community of global partners and followers
